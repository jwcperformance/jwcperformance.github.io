03 — approach

Google Ads for B2B and B2C — both need a different approach.

Google Ads works differently depending on whether you're selling to businesses or consumers. The intent signals, bidding strategy, keyword structure and conversion tracking that works for B2C lead generation won't work as-is for a B2B sales cycle — and vice versa.

B2C Google Ads

High-volume, lower cost-per-lead

Fast buying cycles, higher search volumes, competitive keywords. Built around getting the right intent signals and keeping CPL down without sacrificing lead quality.

B2B Google Ads

Longer cycles, higher-value leads

Smaller search volumes, longer decision cycles, multiple stakeholders. Built around the right match types, negative lists and tracking that credits leads properly — not just the last click.
