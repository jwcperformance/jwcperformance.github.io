What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

What cookies does this site use?

JWC Performance uses a small number of cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to third parties.

Cookie Provider Purpose Type Duration
jwc_cookie_consent JWC Performance Stores your cookie consent preference so you're not asked again Necessary 1 year
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics (GA4) Tracks page views and site usage to help improve the site — only set if you accept cookies Analytics 2 years
_gtm* Google Tag Manager Manages the loading of tracking scripts — only analytics tags fire if you accept cookies Analytics Session

Necessary cookies

The `jwc_cookie_consent` cookie is strictly necessary — it stores your consent choice so we don't ask you every time you visit. This cookie does not require your consent to be set.

Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics (GA4) via Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are visited, how long people spend, and where they come from. This data is anonymised and used only to improve the site.

Analytics cookies are only set if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. If you click "Reject", no analytics data is collected.
