What's included

Everything needed to launch with confidence.

The build covers every element required to get a Google Ads account live and performing from day one — not just setting up campaigns, but making sure the foundations are right.

01
Scoping & brief
A structured brief is agreed upfront — covering what you want to achieve, who your core audience is, your budget and your key conversion goals. This drives every decision in the build.
02
Tracking confirmation
Before the build starts, tracking is confirmed. Whether you need to track leads, MQLs, SQLs, purchases or calls — it's all set up correctly so the account can optimise from real data, not guesses.
03
Full keyword research
Comprehensive keyword research covering head terms, long-tail opportunities, competitor terms and negative keywords — mapped to the brief and structured for campaign use.
04
Themed campaigns & ad groups
Campaign and ad group structure built to best practice — themed tightly around intent, with clear separation between different products, services or audiences.
05
Location & demographic targeting
Location targeting, demographic adjustments and audience layering set up based on your brief — so budget is focused where your customers actually are.
06
Ad copy & assets
Responsive search ads written for each ad group, plus all available assets (sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets etc.) set up to maximise ad coverage and quality score.
07
Competitor & market review
A review of what competitors are bidding on and how they position in ads — so your account launches with context, not blind to what it's competing against.
08
Bidding strategy setup
The right bidding strategy selected and configured for your goals and account maturity — with a clear rationale for why, not just a default setting applied.
09
Excel pre-approval
The full account plan — keywords, structure, ad copy, targeting and budget allocation — submitted in Excel for your review and sign-off before anything is built in Google Ads.
10
Launch
Once you've signed off the Excel plan, the account is built in Google Ads and launched. Everything is checked before going live — tracking, budgets, targeting and ad approvals confirmed before spend begins.
