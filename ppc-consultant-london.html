Why location matters less than you think

Google Ads is run from a screen, not a boardroom.

Plenty of businesses across London, Kent and the South East default to searching for a "local" PPC consultant, assuming proximity matters. In truth, Google Ads management happens entirely online. What actually determines results is expertise, communication and how closely someone manages your account, not a shared postcode.

Where the work happens

Inside the Google Ads platform

Campaigns, bidding, keywords and tracking are all managed online. A consultant based two miles away has no practical advantage over one working remotely, provided communication is clear and regular.

What you actually need

Access and accountability

What matters is whether you can reach the person managing your account, whether they understand your sector, and whether reporting is honest and clear. None of that depends on location.

When meeting in person helps

Occasionally, not constantly

Being based in Bromley means an in person meeting across London, Kent or the South East is entirely possible when it's genuinely useful. Most ongoing work happens over call, email and shared reporting instead.

The real risk

Choosing on proximity alone

Picking a consultant purely because they're nearby, rather than because they're genuinely good at Google Ads, is one of the most common and costly mistakes businesses make when hiring.

Yes, that's entirely possible for clients across London, Kent and the wider South East, and can be arranged where it's genuinely useful, particularly early in a working relationship.

No, clients are based right across the UK. London, Kent and the South East are simply where in person meetings are most practical, but the majority of the work is delivered remotely regardless of where a client is based.
